Name Type Facts
Drifloon Ghost/Flying Drifloon, the Balloon Pokémon. Drifloon grab children's hands as if they were going to guide them to the Ghost World. Although Drifloon tug on children's hands, they often end up getting pulled around instead.
Wooper Water/Ground Wooper, the Water Fish Pokémon. Though they prefer to live in cold water, these appealing Pokémon forage for food on land. They are kept warm by a layer of insulating body fluid. Wooper is the pre-evolved form of Quagsire.
Ditto Normal Ditto, the Transform Pokémon. Ditto has the ability to reconstruct its entire cellular structure into whatever it sees.
Mimikyu Ghost/Fairy Mimikyu, the Disguise Pokémon. It wears a ragged head cover to look like a Pikachu, but little is known about this Pokémon. It's said that a scholar who once tried to look inside met his end.
Sableye Dark/Ghost Sableye, the Darkness Pokémon. Sableye normally live deep within caves and eat rocks. They use their sharp claws to dig through the earth.
Rayquaza Dragon/Flying Legendary Rayquaza, the Sky High Pokémon. It has lived for hundreds of millions of years in the ozone layer. When it flies at night, it resembles a shooting star.
